Welcome to the Photo Opp Project

I'm running a little experiment, and I hope you'll be a part of it. Please take a picture of your daily life and send it to me. I'll post it to this website.

The only information I'd like you to provide is where you are in the world (City, Country). You're welcome to write a couple sentences about yourself and/or the photograph too. If you want to be ananomous, that's ok! If you would like credit for the photo please let me know how to give it to you. Could be your name or a link to one of your social accounts.

Please, keep anything you send me PG-13. I will report any photos that contain any illegal content to the approporate authorities.
